      <h1>BomKalkulator</h1>

      <span class="badge">Support</span>


      <p>BomKalkulator helps you calculate toll costs on Norwegian roads. Enter your start and destination and the app finds all toll stations along your route.</p>


      <h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

      <ul>

          <li><strong>Why are some toll prices different from what I pay?</strong><br>

          Prices are sourced from official Norwegian road data (NVDB). Some stations may have a short delay before updated prices appear.</li>


          <li><strong>Does the app work without internet?</strong><br>

          An internet connection is required to fetch route data and toll prices.</li>


          <li><strong>What is BomKalkulator Premium?</strong><br>

          A one-time purchase that unlocks unlimited route searches forever. No subscription.</li>


          <li><strong>How do I restore my purchase?</strong><br>

          Go to Settings inside the app and tap "Restore Purchase".</li>

      </ul>
