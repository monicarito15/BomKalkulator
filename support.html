BomKalkulator

Support

BomKalkulator helps you calculate toll costs on Norwegian roads. Enter your start and destination and the app finds all toll stations along your route.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why are some toll prices different from what I pay?
    Prices are sourced from official Norwegian road data (NVDB). Some stations may have a short delay before updated prices appear.
  • Does the app work without internet?
    An internet connection is required to fetch route data and toll prices.
  • What is BomKalkulator Premium?
    A one-time purchase that unlocks unlimited route searches forever. No subscription.
  • How do I restore my purchase?
    Go to Settings inside the app and tap "Restore Purchase".
